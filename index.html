Peas Stew Recipe

Image referenced from Foodista Pea Stew Recipe

This Stew has been passed down from generations to generations in my family, so not only is it delicious, it is extremely close to the heart! Recently, I've been seeing an uptick of people sharing personal family recipes online so I decided to give it a go!


What You Will Need
Ingrediants Amount
Olive Oil 30 ml or 2 tbs
smoked cured ham, cut into cubes 300 g
Onion(s) Chopped 1
fresh or frozen peas 400 g
tomato purée 400g
water 450 ml
sea salt 1/4 tsp
ground black pepper (or Vegeta Natur) 1/4 tsp
potatoes, peeled and cut into small cubes 400 g
sugar 1 tbs


Preparation

  1. Fry the cubed smoked cured ham in olive oil for about 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Stir in onion and sauté for about 2 minutes.

  2. Add peas, tomato puree, water, salt and pepper or Vegeta Natur, stir to combine the ingredients.

  3. Add potato cubes and a spoonful of sugar at the end. Cover the pot, let the stew boil, then reduce the flame and cook for about 15 minutes or until the potatoes are soft.

Now Enjoy! :]
